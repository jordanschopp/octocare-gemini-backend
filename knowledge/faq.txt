OctoNet Mobility FAQ

What is OctoNet Mobility?
OctoNet Mobility is building a customer-first mobile connectivity brand focused on travel/global data eSIM services.

Is OctoNet a full phone plan replacement?
Not right now. OctoNet currently focuses on data-only travel/global eSIM services.

Does OctoNet include calling, texting, or phone numbers?
No. Current services do not include phone numbers, calling, texting, port-ins, or full mobile phone plan replacement.

Who should use OctoNet?
Travelers, remote workers, students, business users, and people who need flexible data while traveling.

What can OctoCare help with?
OctoCare can help with eSIM questions, device compatibility, travel data questions, activation guidance, plan information, and basic troubleshooting.

How do customers get human support?
Customers should contact hello.octonetmobility@gmail.com.

Launch status:
OctoNet Mobility has a 2027 launch projection.

Important rule:
Do not invent prices, coverage claims, partnerships, calling/texting features, or full carrier services unless they are officially provided in OctoNet documents.
